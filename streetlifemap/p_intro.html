Street life Map

Our project intends to propose a new way of visualizing the quality of urban street life by measuring the intensity of social activities that may happen on each street. The score is calculated by certain types of geo-spatial point (POI) data, for instance, catering, leisure, shop, and tree cover ratio. The Streetlife map encourages citizens to explore the city for their everyday life experience rather than searching for the shortest routes from one place to another.
